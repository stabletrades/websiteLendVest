What is Lend Vest ?

Lend Vest is a platform that's powering data for the emerging credit system in DeFi

Currently, there is no establised credit score that provides material benifits to crypto users.

Our aim is to create & aggregate financial APIs to form the basis of an opem standard credit score for oracle operators.

The Lend Vest Governance Token (LV) will represnt ownership in the oracle netwrk relaying this transformative open standard credit score.
